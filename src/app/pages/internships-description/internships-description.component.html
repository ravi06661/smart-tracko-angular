As a Graphic Designer, you will play a crucial role in translating concepts and ideas into visually stunning designs. You will collaborate closely with our marketing team, clients, and other stakeholders to ensure that design objectives are met and that our brand identity is maintained consistently across different platforms.

Responsibilities:

  • Create visually compelling designs for marketing campaigns, including but not limited to digital advertisements, social media graphics, website elements, and email templates.
  • Develop and design eye-catching branding materials, such as logos, business cards, brochures, and signage.
  • Collaborate with the marketing team to understand project requirements and objectives, ensuring that design solutions align with the overall marketing strategy.
  • Translate complex ideas and concepts into visually engaging infographics and illustrations.
  • Maintain a high level of creativity while adhering to brand guidelines and ensuring consistency in design elements.
  • Stay updated with industry trends and emerging design techniques to continuously improve design quality and effectiveness.
  • Manage multiple projects simultaneously and meet deadlines without compromising on quality or attention to detail.
  • Prepare design files for print and digital distribution, ensuring proper formatting, resolution, and color accuracy.
  • Work closely with clients, internal teams, and external vendors to ensure effective communication and collaboration throughout the design process.
  • Contribute to brainstorming sessions and provide valuable input in the ideation and conceptualization of design projects.
